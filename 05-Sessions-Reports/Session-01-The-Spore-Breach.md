# Session 1: The Spore Breach

## Recap
The heavy iron gates of Havenport slammed shut, locking the party outside in the creeping, mustard-yellow fog of the Eastern Swamp. A terrified Concord druid burst from the treeline, closely pursued by a colossal, mutating Giant Boar, throwing the party immediately into a fight for their lives.

## Session Summary
- The party successfully dispatched the Blight-Maddened Giant Boar at the gates of Havenport.
- Concord Druid Nessa Glowroot hired the party (for 50 gp) to delve into the swamp and destroy a cluster of Blight-Pods before the wind could carry their toxic spores into the city.
- The party successfully navigated a 40-foot expanse of deep, toxic, freezing muck.
- The party discovered Silas, a Dominion surveyor, pinned beneath a collapsed metal wagon. Despite suspicions and faction tensions, they chose to heal him and let him go.
- Reached the Blight-Pod cluster, which was mind-controlled a Blight-Maddened Owlbear. The party tactically focused their attacks on the spores and tethering vines, successfully freeing the Owlbear without having to kill it.
- On the trek back, the party caught up with a limping Silas and escorted him back to the Havenport gates.
- Upon arrival, Dominion guards quickly whisked Silas away, preventing the party from investigating him further or handing him over to the Concord druids.
- The mission was a complete success, and the party received their 50 gp reward.
