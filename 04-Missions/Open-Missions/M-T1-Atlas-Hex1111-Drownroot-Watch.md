# Mission: A Quiet Watch on the Drownroot

## The Call to Action
Faelynn Wildheart does not post this on a board. She invites the party — quietly, by name, through a green-leafed token left at their lodging — to walk with her in the herb gardens behind the **Verdant Sigil** at first light. Once there, she speaks plainly:

> *"There is a place at the northeast edge of our reach — a fen, with a deep pool at its heart called Drownroot Hollow. Acorns are falling there from trees that bear no oaks. They are warm. They hum. My scouts have placed a sigil of Keeping on the cypress arch at the Hollow's approach, and we have been... encouraging the local wildlife to keep visitors from wading. None of this is yet meant to be public.*
>
> *I am asking you to go and watch — not to take, not to disturb, only to watch — at dusk, from a place I will mark for you, and to tell me what you hear beneath the frog-song. Bring me one acorn, intact, still warm, if one drifts to your hand. Do not enter the water of the Hollow. The alligator is not the worst thing in that pool."*
>
> — *Faelynn Wildheart, Verdant Seat*

She names a local forager, **[[../../01-Campaign-Bible/Entities/NPCs/Woody-Haygrin|Woody Haygrin]]**, who keeps a blind at the fen's edge and will permit the party to use it if they show the green-leafed token. She is firm: this is a *listening* mission. Steel will be the wrong tool.

## Objectives
1. **Primary — Listen at the Hollow:** Travel to [[../../03-Hex-Map/Region-01-Safe-Zone/Hex-1111|Hex 1111: Drownroot Hollow]], present Faelynn's token to **Woody Haygrin**, take position in his blind at dusk, and report back what is heard beneath the frog-song from the direction of the **Drownroot Hollow** pool. Bring back **one intact, still-warm acorn from the Acorn Fall** — drawn off the surface from the bank, **without entering the water** and **without disturbing the giant alligator** at its kill. *(Direct combat with the alligator will void the patron's Primary payout — the entire point is that this place be left undisturbed.)*
2. **Denial / Secondary — Recover the Concord Arrow & Pull the Lure:** Faelynn knows one of her scouts drove the deer into the water as bait. She does not want **Cronus Dominion surveyors** or **Hyperion Syndicate prospectors** finding the **green-fletched arrow** in the half-eaten carcass and reading the same pattern she did. Recover the arrow from the **Drag-Furrows** kill site, return it to her, and drag what remains of the deer carcass into the deeper water for the alligator to finish — erasing the lure trail. This denies a clue to both rival factions and advances the Concord's *secrecy* counter on the Oakharrow clock.

## Reward Distribution Structure
- **Faction Points (FP):** **1 FP × 1** to Atlas Concord for Primary completion. **+1 FP** if Denial is also achieved (max **2 FP**). *(Awarded to participating characters' aligned factions; FP only paid if the party did not enter the water of the Hollow.)*
- **Bastion Points (BP):** **2 BP** to **Havenport (Verdant Seat / Atlas Concord)** on Primary. **+1 BP** if Denial is also achieved (max **3 BP**). *(BP source: a single intact warm acorn delivered to the Verdant Sigil for study is worth a tier of Concord goodwill on its own — it is, in Faelynn's quiet judgement, the first physical evidence she has had of what she has long believed.)*
- **Individual Rewards:** **50 gp** per character on Primary completion (Faelynn pays in honest Havenport coin and a single dose of Concord-grown *goodberry*-equivalent rations per character — 4 berries, full day's nutrition each). **+25 gp per character** if Denial is achieved (max **75 gp**).
- **Penalty Clause:** If the party **enters the water of the Hollow** for any reason, or if they **disturb the alligator at its kill**, the Primary FP and BP are forfeit and gold is reduced to **25 gp per character** for the trouble taken. Faelynn does not punish honest mistakes, but she will not pay for them.
- **Loot In-Hex (kept by party):** **The copper dockmaster's ring** on the abandoned stilt-camp piling and any **non-acorn flotsam** in the camp are the party's. The fist-sized intact warm acorn at the bottom of the Hollow itself is **off-limits** under the terms of this mission — Faelynn will know if it is missing.

## GM Notes & The "B-Plot" Clue
*(Read silently. Do not reveal to players.)*

- **The Real Motive:** Faelynn's Oakharrow clock is at Step 1/6 — *locating the dormant root system*. She has located it. What she does **not** yet have is independent corroboration that a non-Concord witness can hear and feel what her druids hear and feel at the Hollow. She is using the party as an *un-trained* sensor: if outsiders, with no ear for ley-lines, can perceive the vegetal groan beneath the frogs, then the root-system is closer to waking than her scouts have admitted. The mission is also a deliberate **counter-reconnaissance** — the green-fletched arrow is sloppy tradecraft, and she wants it gone before a Dominion or Syndicate party finds it.
- **The B-Plot Truth (Oakharrow tie):** The acorns are not seeds. They are **Oakharrow's surface — its rind, slowly sloughing as it stirs.** Each warm acorn is a piece of a primordial guardian the size of a buried mountain range. The root system extends west under the wood of [[../../03-Hex-Map/Region-01-Safe-Zone/Hex-1011|Hex 1011]] — it is the same root that pushed up the flagstone of the Old Haven Road and bent the Dominion's blaze-line by half a degree. **Oakharrow is not local. Oakharrow is everywhere underneath them.** Faelynn suspects this. She has not yet said it aloud, even to her own druids.
- **The Concrete Clue (one, plant where the party will find it):** At dusk, from Woody's blind, beneath the silence of the frogs and the slow groan from the Hollow, the party will hear — clearly, if they listen for thirty heartbeats — **the same low, slow vegetal groan answering, faintly, from the southwest.** Southwest is the direction of the Brineglass Wood and the Dominion blaze-line. A character with even a passing knowledge of [[../../03-Hex-Map/Region-01-Safe-Zone/Hex-1011|Hex 1011]] (or who has walked it) will realize the second groan is coming from beneath the **Listening Stump**. The roots are connected. The wood was *listening* on purpose.
- **The Acorn in Faelynn's Hand:** When the party delivers the warm acorn at the Verdant Sigil, Faelynn will hold it for a long moment, close her eyes, and say only: *"Thank you. We are not as alone as we feared."* She will then change the subject. Mark on the Oakharrow clock that the Concord now has its first independent confirmation; this is canonically the moment Step 1/6 fully fills.
- **If the party kills the alligator:** Faelynn pays the penalty rate without comment, but a Concord scout will quietly trail the party for the next two missions. The Concord's lure is gone; the Hollow is now defended only by reputation, and the acorns at the bottom are correspondingly more vulnerable to the next Syndicate prospector who walks in.
- **If the party takes the bottom-of-the-Hollow fist-sized acorn anyway:** They have stolen a piece of a primordial. It will not stop humming. Sleep becomes difficult while it is on their person. Faelynn will know within a day. This is the seed of a Tier 2 reckoning, not a Tier 1 mission.
