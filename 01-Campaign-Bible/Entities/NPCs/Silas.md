# Silas

**Role**: Surveyor
**Faction**: [[Cronus Dominion]]

## Overview
Silas is a surveyor for the [[Cronus Dominion]] who was discovered by the party during their first venture into the [[Hex-1110|Blighted Fens]] (Eastern Swamp). He was found in a dire state, pinned beneath a collapsed metal wagon amidst toxic muck and creeping blight spores. 

A man of few words, Silas possesses the pragmatic and disciplined air typical of Dominion personnel. While he appeared rattled by his ordeal, he maintained a professional reserve, only offering the party a heavy iron lockbox as a token of gratitude for his rescue.

## Session History
- **[[Session-01-The-Spore-Breach]]**: Rescued by the party after being found pinned under a metal wagon in the swamp. Despite the party's suspicions, they healed his injuries and escorted him back to the gates of [[Havenport]]. Upon arrival, he was immediately intercepted and whisked away by Dominion guards before the party could question him further.

## GM Notes / Hooks
- **The Metal Wagon**: What was a Dominion surveyor doing in the deep swamp with a heavy metal wagon? The secrecy of the guards who retrieved him suggests the "surveying" mission may have been a cover for something more sensitive.
- **The Lockbox**: Silas gave the party a heavy iron lockbox containing 2 healing potions and 1 [[Dominion Seal]]. This seal may grant the party leverage or access in future dealings with the Dominion.
- **Gratitude vs. Duty**: While Silas is personally grateful, his first loyalty is to [[Commander-Valerius]] and the Dominion. If the party's interests ever conflict with the Dominion's order, Silas may find himself in a difficult position.
