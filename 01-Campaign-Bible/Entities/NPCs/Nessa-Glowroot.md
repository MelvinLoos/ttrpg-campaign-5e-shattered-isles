# Nessa Glowroot

**Role**: Druid
**Faction**: [[Atlas-Concord|Atlas Concord]]

## Overview
Nessa Glowroot is a dedicated druid of the [[Atlas-Concord|Atlas Concord]], committed to maintaining the delicate balance of the Shattered Isles. She first encountered the party at the gates of [[Havenport]], emerging from the treeline in a desperate flight from a Blight-Maddened Giant Boar. 

Nessa is deeply attuned to the natural world and views the spreading blight not just as a physical threat, but as a spiritual wound upon the land. She is proactive and observant, often noticing environmental shifts long before they reach the city walls.

## Session History
- **[[Session-01-The-Spore-Breach]]**: Hired the party for 50 gp to destroy a cluster of Blight-Pods in the Eastern Swamp ([[Hex-1110]]) that threatened to release toxic spores into [[Havenport]]. She was grateful for the party's swift action in neutralizing the threat and saving a mind-controlled Owlbear.

## GM Notes / Hooks
- **Repeat Patron**: As a boots-on-the-ground agent for the [[Atlas-Concord|Atlas Concord]], Nessa is a primary source for missions involving ecological preservation, research, and blight containment.
- **Awaken Oakharrow**: Nessa's network of druids is highly sensitive to the pulse of the forest. She is a key NPC for advancing clocks related to [[Oakharrow]] and the Concord's long-term goals.
- **Faction Liaison**: She serves as a friendly bridge to [[Faelynn-Wildheart]] and the higher tiers of the Concord leadership.
