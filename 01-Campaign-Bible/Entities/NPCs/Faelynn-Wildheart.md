# Faelynn Wildheart

**Occupation**: Archdruid & Faction Leader

**Appearance**: Weather-beaten Wood Elf, living vine braids, ironwood armor

**Personality**: Intense, soft-spoken, protective of nature, wary of industry

**Background**: Guardian of the Wilds seeking to heal the Sundered Isles

> "The Isles do not need to be conquered, they need to be heard. If you are here to break the stone and burn the wood, you will find no friend in me. But if you are here to listen? Then come, sit. The forest has much to say."

Faelynn Wildheart acts as the spiritual and operational heart of the **[[Atlas-Concord|Atlas Concord]]**. She is rarely found behind a desk; instead, she is usually seen tending to the living architecture of Havenport or mediating disputes between settlers and the local fey.

She is a Wood Elf with skin the color of polished oak and hair like mossy river stones, often braided with living vines or feathers found on her travels. She dresses in practical leathers reinforced with ironwood rather than metal, moving with a predator's grace and a sage's patience. A distinct scent follows her—petrichor, pine needles, and ozone.

Despite the wildness of her name, Faelynn is not a savage. She is soft-spoken and deliberate, possessing an intense, unblinking stare that seems to weigh a person's soul against the natural order. She champions the ideology that the Isles must be understood, not domesticated, seeking harmony with the awakened beings of the land.

### The Guide & The Warden

Faelynn serves as the primary contact for adventurers seeking to understand the Shattered Isles rather than just exploit them. She represents the Atlas Concord's goal to restore balance to the wild energies of the land.

As a quest giver, Faelynn focuses on **Exploration**. She tasks adventurers with mapping unknown hexes, communing with awakened beasts, or cleansing areas corrupted by wild magic. She is also the gatekeeper to the Concord's unique resources, overseeing the growth of magical gardens and the care of animal companions within the Havenport.

## Council Role
Holds the **Verdant Seat (Sustenance)** on the [[Havenport-Concordat|Havenport Concordat]].
