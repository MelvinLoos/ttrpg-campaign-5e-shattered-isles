# NPC Index

A lookup table of all known NPCs in the Shattered Isles campaign. Individual files live in `01-Campaign-Bible/Entities/NPCs/`.

## Havenport — Concordat Leadership
- [[NPCs/Magistrate-Theron|Magistrate Theron]]: Pragmatic head of the Havenport Concordat; holder of the High Seat and arbiter of the three-faction council.
- [[NPCs/Commander-Valerius|Commander Valerius]]: Iron-willed military commander of the Cronus Dominion; holder of the Iron Seat on the Concordat.
- [[NPCs/Lady-Vexis|Lady Vexis]]: Calculating Tiefling Merchant Princess leading the Hyperion Syndicate; holder of the Gilded Seat on the Concordat.
- [[NPCs/Faelynn-Wildheart|Faelynn Wildheart]]: Wood Elf Archdruid leading the Atlas Concord; holder of the Verdant Seat on the Concordat.

## Havenport — Notable Residents
- [[NPCs/Brother-Caelum-Ash|Brother Caelum Ash]]: Patient ash-haired librarian who keeps Havenport's recorded knowledge at The Common Record.
- [[NPCs/Garrick-Stonevein|Garrick Stonevein]]: Gruff, scarred dwarf monster hunter who operates out of The Broken Trail.
- [[NPCs/Kaela-Runeforge|Kaela Runeforge]]: Reserved human blacksmith whose reliable work defines The Ember Anvil.
- [[NPCs/Marla-Swiftpour|Marla Swiftpour]]: Steady tavernkeeper of The Last Lantern and quiet orientation point for newcomers.
- [[NPCs/Nessa-Glowroot|Nessa Glowroot]]: Gentle orc arcanist of The Verdant Sigil who treats magic as a relationship to be maintained.
- [[NPCs/Pip-Underbough|Pip Underbough]]: Approachable halfling shopkeeper of The Steady Supply, focused on practical preparation.

## Atlas Concord Agents
- [[NPCs/Lyra-Meadowlight|Lyra Meadowlight]]: Atlas Concord researcher who handled the first samples of the Blight gathered by one of the adventuring parties.

## Independents & Wanderers
- [[NPCs/Jaquess-Le-Baron-Samedi|Jaquess "Le Baron" Samedi]]: Masked, immaculately dressed wandering alchemist and occult banker who lends gold on unsettling terms.
- [[NPCs/Woody-Haygrin|Woody Haygrin]]: Halfling forager who keeps a blind at the edge of Drownroot Hollow (Hex 1111) and warns travelers off its dangerous waters.

## Missing & Unaccounted For
- [[NPCs/Edbert-Thurlow|Edbert Thurlow]]: Havenport fisherman missing nine days; his wrecked dory lies buried on Gullcry Strand with stamped Dominion nails inside the hull.

## Mythic / Unseen
- [[NPCs/Oakharrow|Oakharrow]]: Primordial guardian said to slumber deep within the earth, known only through Faelynn Wildheart's stories.
