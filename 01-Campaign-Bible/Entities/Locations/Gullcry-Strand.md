# Gullcry Strand

**Type**: Coastal Beach and Salvage Ground

East of Havenport, the soft harbor beaches give way to a long, curving strand of black shingle, broken pillars of sea-cut basalt, and tide pools that drain with a loud suck between swells. The beach takes its name from the perpetual white sheet of gulls that works the tideline — lifting, wheeling, and resettling without cease.

The strand is a working salvage-ground for Havenport fishers and beachcombers. Rope, staved barrels, and broken spar-wood come ashore with the tides, and modest finds are common. More valuable finds are traded quietly; the dockmaster asks few questions about what the sea returns.

At low tide, a flat of wet black sand opens toward **the Teeth**, a line of submerged reef that has wrecked many a ship in fog. Locals give it a wide berth and will point it out, unprompted, to any newcomer.

## Notable Features
- **The Teeth** — submerged reef line, exposed at low tide.
- **The Keeled Dory** — a wrecked fishing boat belonging to [[../NPCs/Edbert-Thurlow|Edbert Thurlow]], missing nine days; its keel is split too cleanly to be reef-damage.
- **The Cairn Above The Tide** — a fresh, low cairn concealing two crates of raw Blight-crystals planted by Hyperion Syndicate smugglers.
