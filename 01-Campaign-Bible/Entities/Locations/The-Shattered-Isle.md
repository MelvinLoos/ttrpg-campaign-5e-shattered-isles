# The Shattered Isle

**Type**: World

### **The Heroes' Guild Presents: The Shattered Isles**

Welcome, pioneer, to the **Heroes' Guild** and the untamed lands of the **Shattered Isles**. Your journey begins in a world that was not born, but broken.

Long ago, a cataclysm known as **the Sundering** tore the old world asunder, reshaping the continent into a vast, spiraling archipelago of raw, untamed magic. In its wake, a powerful **Divine Covenant** was forged: true death is forbidden within the Isles. The fallen are recalled and reborn, their souls returning to the nearest **Bastion**, a fortified settlement that serves as both a safe haven and a frontier outpost.

You are one of the brave adventurers who have answered the call to explore this new world. As a member of the Heroes' Guild, you are not a lone hero, but a vital part of a grand expedition. The campaign is a **West Marches-style adventure**, which means your mission is not to follow a single linear story, but to venture out from the safety of Havenport, explore the unknown, and write the history of the Isles as you go.

The world is living, breathing, and dangerous. Three powerful factions vie for control, but not with open war. Their conflict is a tense dance of politics, resources, and influence, with the fate of the Isles hanging in the balance:

* **[[Atlas-Concord|The Atlas Concord]]** seeks harmony with the land's primal magic.
* **[[Cronus-Dominion|The Cronus Dominion]]** strives to bring order to the chaos.
* **[[Hyperion-Syndicate|The Hyperion Syndicate]]** sees a world ripe for profitable exploitation.

Your character will be a direct agent in this struggle. Your actions will determine which faction gains power, which secrets are uncovered, and whether the Isles can be truly settled, or if their wild magic will consume all who dare to enter.

The world beyond Havenport's walls is waiting to be discovered. Which path will you forge?
