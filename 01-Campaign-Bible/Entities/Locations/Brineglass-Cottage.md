# Brineglass Cottage

**Type**: Abandoned Alchemist's Cottage

A slate-roofed, single-room cottage set a short way off the old Haven Road, in a clearing among salt-tolerant pines. Its shutters are closed but unlatched, its chimney is cold, and its garden has been going feral for several weeks. The cottage is not ransacked. Someone left it in an orderly hurry.

Inside, bundles of dried herbs still hang from the rafters — sea-lavender, bitter-root, and a third bundle of something no one in Havenport has been able to name. A copper distillation rig stands on a stone bench, cold but clean. A kettle sits empty on the hearth. On a writing-board by the shuttered window, a half-finished letter has been left under a stone weight; the salutation reads "B.C.A." and the last legible line is "— if you receive this and the bearer is not me, please understand I did not —".

Under a loose floorboard near the hearth lies a small iron strongbox.

## Notable Contents
- 2 doses of *antitoxin* in stoppered vials.
- 1 half-finished letter addressed to "B.C.A." (likely [[../NPCs/Brother-Caelum-Ash|Brother Caelum Ash]]).
- 1 locked strongbox (DC 13 Thieves' Tools): 14 gp, a silver stirring rod, a pressed leaf of an unidentified herb.
