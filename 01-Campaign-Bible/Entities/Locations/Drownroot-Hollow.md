# Drownroot Hollow

**Type**: Tidal Fen Pool

Drownroot Hollow is the deep-water heart of the brackish fen northeast of Havenport. Ringed with moss-beard cypress and the pale, finger-like knees of their roots, the Hollow breathes with the tides of the nearby coast: rising at dusk, falling at dawn, leaving a crust of salt-rime on every trunk.

The Hollow is, by any practical measure, dangerous. Its water is dark, its footing treacherous, and a very large alligator is presently living in it. Frogs fall silent within sixty paces. The usual clouds of biting mosquitos are conspicuously absent — a fact that an old fen-hand will read as a warning, not a mercy.

More recently, something else has begun to stir the Hollow. Unripe, warm-to-the-touch acorns drift in slow, deliberate patterns on its surface, though no oak grows within a mile. A druidic sigil of Keeping has been freshly burned into the great cypress arch at the Hollow's approach, and Atlas Concord scouts are no longer a rumor along its banks. At dusk, a slow vegetal groan — like great timber shifting under its own weight — can sometimes be heard beneath the frog-song, from deep under the water.

## Notable Features
- **The Cypress Gate** — a natural arch of two grown-together cypresses; inner face bears a fresh druidic sigil of Keeping.
- **The Acorn Fall** — humming, unseasonal acorns floating against the current.
- **The Drag-Furrows** — the alligator's feeding trails into the water.
- **The Fisher's Stilt-Camp** — a collapsed fishing platform; a single laced boot in the shallows.

## Key NPCs
- [[../NPCs/Woody-Haygrin|Woody Haygrin]] — forager who keeps a blind at the fen's edge and warns travelers off the Hollow.
