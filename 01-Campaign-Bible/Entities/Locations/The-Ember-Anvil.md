# The Ember Anvil

**Type**: Armory

> **"Nothing forged here promises victory, only that it will not fail you."**

The Ember Anvil stands close enough to Havenport's center to hear the city breathe, yet far enough from its quieter spaces that the sound of hammer on steel does not intrude. From the outside, the forge appears unadorned, its stone walls darkened by years of heat and smoke. The glow from within is steady rather than dramatic.

Inside, the Ember Anvil is warm, bright, and unmistakably active. The forge itself dominates the space, its embers carefully maintained. Tools hang in deliberate order along the walls, and unfinished pieces rest where they can be seen and assessed rather than hidden away. The air smells of heated metal, oil, and ash, and the rhythm of work gives the space its own tempo.

The Ember Anvil exists to ensure that Havenport's adventurers are properly equipped for what lies beyond its walls. It is a place of preparation rather than invention, where equipment is adapted to purpose and limitations are discussed openly. Those who enter without a clear request are often encouraged to return once they know what they need.

Visitors to the Ember Anvil can expect practical assessments of their gear, honest advice about its condition, and access to weapons and armor built to endure sustained use. While the forge is capable of exceptional craftsmanship, it is not a place of spectacle. Items produced here are valued for reliability rather than ornamentation.

[[Kaela-Runeforge|Kaela Runeforge]]'s presence defines the Ember Anvil completely. Her standards shape both the work produced and the expectations placed upon those who commission it. When she is absent, the forge remains operational, but its pace slows, as if unwilling to rush without her oversight.

Among those who have traveled the Shattered Isles, the Ember Anvil is known as a place where equipment is treated with the same seriousness as the lives that depend on it. It is often said that weapons forged here do not seek glory — they simply do not fail.
