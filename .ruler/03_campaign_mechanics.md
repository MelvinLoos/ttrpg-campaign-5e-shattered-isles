# The Shattered Isles Core Mechanics
When generating missions, NPCs, or Bastion events, adhere to these West Marches mechanics:

1. **The Faction War:** There are 3 primary factions vying for control. Their conflict is non-violent—it is driven by economics, politics, and exploration. 
2. **Faction Points (FP) & Bastion Points (BP):** Missions reward 1 FP (multiplied by x1, x2, x3) to aligned factions, and BP to the hosting Bastion. Every mission must state these rewards clearly.
3. **Mission Structure:** Every mission MUST have a "Primary Objective" and a "Denial/Secondary Objective".

# Encounter Scaling & Tiers of Renown (Tier 1 Focus)
The current campaign scope is locked to **Tier 1 Renown**. You must scale generated content based on the "Concentric Danger Zones" radiating from a Bastion:

- **Zone 1 (1-3 Hexes out):** Primarily Tier 1 threats (CR 0 to CR 2). Goblins, local wildlife, minor elemental anomalies, and bandit scouts.
- **Zone 2 (4-6 Hexes out):** Hints of Tier 2 threats.
- **Telegraphing Danger (MANDATORY):** Because this is a sandbox, higher-level creatures *can* exist near the players, but you MUST heavily telegraph their presence so players have an active choice to avoid them. If you place a Tier 2+ threat in a Zone 1 hex, you must describe environmental warnings (e.g., massive claw marks, a slaughtered owlbear, unnatural localized weather) rather than springing a surprise attack.
