# Shattered Isles Encounters by Biome (Tier 1 / Zone 1)

*AI Directive: When generating a Hex or Mission, roll 2d12 on the appropriate Biome table below. Find the exact number and use that encounter. If the result is a creature, you MUST scale its stat block to Tier 1 (CR 0 to CR 2). Remember that results at the extreme ends of the table (2-4) are often deadly and MUST be heavily telegraphed rather than forcing an immediate ambush.*
