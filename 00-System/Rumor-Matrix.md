# Rumor Matrix

*AI Directive: Rumors are the lifeblood of the West Marches sandbox. Each hex seeds three rumors: one **True**, one **Partial** (a true core wrapped in a misleading frame), and one **False**. Rumors are heard in taverns, on market day, from guards, from the drunk at the next table, from children. The GM decides who carries which.*

## Hex 1011 — Brineglass Wood (Forest, North of Havenport)
- **True:** "The Dominion's driving iron nails into the trees north of town. Straight line, chest-height, stamped with the sun. Somebody on the Verdant Seat is going to *taste blood* when they hear about it." — *Dockhand, off-shift, at the Last Lantern.*
- **Partial:** "Old Widow Brineglass up the north road took in sick and fled back to the mainland — left her whole shop standing, door open. Poor woman." — *Market gossip.* *(True core: the cottage is abandoned and intact. Misleading frame: there is no "Widow Brineglass," the owner's identity is unknown, and she did not leave by choice nor necessarily for "the mainland." The letter on her desk was addressed to Brother Caelum Ash.)*
- **False:** "Wolves have come back to the North Wood. Pack of twelve, moving together, killed a Dominion surveyor last week." — *A hunter, loudly, at the Last Lantern.* *(No wolf pack. No dead surveyor. A Dominion surveyor is under threat in the wood, but from a person, not a pack.)*

## Hex 1110 — Gullcry Strand (Coast, East of Havenport)
- **True:** "Someone's caching goods above the tide on Gullcry. Fresh stone on the sea-grass line. Gulls don't settle over it. If you want my guess, it's not salt-pork." — *A beachcomber, sober, selling mended rope at market.*
- **Partial:** "Edbert Thurlow's dory came apart on the Teeth nine days ago. Fog, bad tide, that's the east shore for you." — *Dockmaster's ledger, repeated by the dockmaster.* *(True core: Edbert's dory is wrecked on the east shore and he is missing. Misleading frame: the boat did not strike the Teeth — the keel was split cleanly, and Dominion-stamped nails were left inside the hull.)*
- **False:** "Pirates out of the eastern isles landed at Gullcry last moon and burned a fishing family out of their cottage. Concordat's keeping it quiet." — *A drunk, very earnestly, to anyone who will listen.* *(There are no pirates on Gullcry. There is no burned cottage. The real activity is a Syndicate cache, much quieter and much more deliberate.)*

## Hex 1111 — Drownroot Hollow (Swamp, Northeast of Havenport)
- **True:** "The fen off the northeast road has gone wrong. Acorns floating on water where no oak grows, and they're *warm* when you pick one up. Woody Haygrin'll tell you the same if you ask him plain." — *A Concord-leaning herbalist at market.*
- **Partial:** "There's a big gator in the Drownroot that's been taking deer. Concord druids put a ward on the cypress gate to keep it penned in — it's the safest it's been in years." — *A retired guard at the Last Lantern.* *(True core: there is a large alligator at Drownroot Hollow, and the Concord has burned a sigil into the cypress arch. Misleading frame: the sigil is not a containment ward on the gator — it's a boundary-marker keeping people out, and the Concord may in fact be feeding the gator to reinforce the boundary.)*
- **False:** "The fen's full of goblins on stilts, harvesting frogs for some witch further inland. Three of them jumped a trapper last week." — *A boatwright, repeating his cousin's story.* *(No goblins on stilts at Drownroot. The stilt-camp in the hex is a collapsed human fishing platform, weeks abandoned.)*
