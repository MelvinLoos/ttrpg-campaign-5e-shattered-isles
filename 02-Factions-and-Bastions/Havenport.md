# Bastion: Havenport

## Overview
Havenport is the central hub of the Shattered Isles and the starting safe zone for all pioneers of the Heroes' Guild. It is the fortified settlement to which the fallen are recalled under the Divine Covenant, serving as both sanctuary and frontier outpost. Though theoretically neutral, Havenport is in practice a tense battleground of influence between the three great factions, held together only by the **[[Havenport-Concordat|Havenport Concordat]]** — the tripartite council that governs the settlement and keeps open warfare from breaking out within its walls.

Its walls shelter the full range of goods, services, and knowledge the Isles have to offer, and every named establishment within Havenport (the Last Lantern, the Common Record, the Ember Anvil, the Broken Trail, the Steady Supply, and the Verdant Sigil) falls under the Concordat's jurisdiction.

## Governance
Havenport is governed by the **[[Havenport-Concordat|Havenport Concordat]]**, which meets in the Chamber of Echoes within the Town Hall. The Concordat consists of four seats:
- **High Seat (Executive):** [[Magistrate-Theron|Magistrate Theron]] — neutral arbiter, holds the tie-breaking vote.
- **Iron Seat (Defense):** [[Commander-Valerius|Commander Valerius]] — [[Cronus-Dominion|Cronus Dominion]].
- **Gilded Seat (Treasury):** [[Lady-Vexis|Lady Vexis]] — [[Hyperion-Syndicate|Hyperion Syndicate]].
- **Verdant Seat (Sustenance):** [[Faelynn-Wildheart|Faelynn Wildheart]] — [[Atlas-Concord|Atlas Concord]].
