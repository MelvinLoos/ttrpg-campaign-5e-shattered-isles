# Cronus Dominion

## Overview
To the Cronus Dominion, the chaos of the Shattered Isles is an enemy to be conquered, and order is the only path to prosperity. Under the command of the stern and pragmatic Commander Valerius, this faction is built on military discipline and a clear hierarchy. They see the wild magic and dangerous creatures of the land as threats to be contained, controlled, and, if necessary, eliminated. Their members, a mix of soldiers, engineers, and city-builders, are focused on establishing secure outposts, building strong fortifications, and creating a stable, predictable society. The Dominion values pragmatism and efficiency above all else, and their ultimate goal is to bring a new age of law.
